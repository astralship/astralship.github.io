What is Astralship?

Astralship is a project to create a visionary facility for Creativity, Innovation and Collaboration.

We are leveraging state of the art developments in fields from Architecture and IoT, to Psychology and advanced manufacturing to repurpose an old Chapel in Snowdonia

The new space will enable teams reaching their full potential and change the world.

Pirate ships and Innovation

We are building a facility where developers, designers, engineers and radical thinkers work together in flow states uninterrupted by the outside world.

Steve Jobs said it's better to be a Pirate than join the navy. We like naval metaphors too expect references to pirates, sailing, voyages, storms, treasure and discovering new lands.

Changemakers don't accept the status quo, they go out and make it happen. The ship provides everything a crew needs to go on innovation journeys.

Total Immersion

An optimised physical and psychological space for crews to channel passion and tackle the hard problems:

  • No barriers: All immediate physical and psychological needs of the crew met
  • No distractions: An inspiring and self contained environment to support the crew
  • Space and support: Nutritious food and exercise, beautiful surrounding to hike, relax and unwind.

A inspiring and exciting space

In the Chapel there is plenty of room to work with. We've already built a prototype ship and we attest that it's a magical space. Now we're upgrading and sharing with the world. See also refurbishment.

We are creating magical and inspiring interior with high-quality living, working and recreational spaces that suggest adventure on even the most serious and focussed of voyages.

The Voyages

We are building a ship, but once built where will we sail? We have a number of revenue models; sometimes we'll assemble specialist crews to tackle our innovation challenges, sometimes we'll lease it out entirely. The ship will allow the bravest and brightest to push themselves and expand their limits.

Some voyages will be very intense, lasting up to 2 weeks, and some crews will need specialist facilitation to reach their goals. On a voyage, start-up teams will design and prototype new products or services, work that might have taken many months under traditional conditions.

Read more about voyages.

  • Affordable services

    In the convential paradigm, getting a team together to work on ideas is expensive and time consuming. The big money investors decide on the projects and keep the rewards.
